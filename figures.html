Key Figures of Six-Day War

Political leaders

Levi Eshkol

Prime Minister of Israel during the Six-Day War. He authorized the preemptive strike and led Israel through a tense political and military crisis.

Gamal Nasser

President of Egypt and leader of the Arab coalition. His decision to close the Straits of Tiran was one of the catalysts of the war.

Military Commanders

Yitzhak Rabin

Chief of Staff of the IDF during the war. Rabin played a central role in planning and executing Israel’s military operations

Moshe Dayan

Minister of Defence of Israel, know for his leadership and iconic eye patch. He became a symbol of Israeli strength.
