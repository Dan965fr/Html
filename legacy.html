Legacy of the Six-Day War

Long-Term Impact

The Six-Day War changed the political and geographic landscape of the Middle East. Israel's control of Jerusalem, the West Bank, Gaza Strip, Sinai Peninsula, and Golan Heights continues to shape regional dynamics and global diplomacy.
